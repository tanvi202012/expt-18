A Quiet Moment

The sun dips low, the skies turn blue,

As evening whispers soft and true.

The stars begin to make their way,

Lighting up the skies of gray.

The wind sings gently through the trees,

While the night hums with such ease.

In the quiet, peace does reign,

As all the world forgets its pain.

Rest your heart, let worries go,

For in this night, all will be slow.
